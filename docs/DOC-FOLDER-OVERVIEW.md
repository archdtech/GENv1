## Project Name & Version
**Project:** MetaBite - DNA Nutrition & Lifestyle App  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Last Updated:** September 2024

## Brief Description
MetaBite is a comprehensive web application that helps users personalize their eating and movement behavior based on their DNA profile. The application combines genetic insights with real-time tracking to provide personalized nutrition recommendations, activity suggestions, and habit formation support. Users can upload their DNA data, log meals and activities, track their progress through streaks, and receive educational content tailored to their genetic profile.
